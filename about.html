About Croosly

Hi, I'm Jóhann.

Croosly started as a side project for my girlfriend. She finishes the New York Times mini before her coffee cools and then has nothing to do, so I built her a small crossword in a single HTML file. She liked it. I kept adding.

Croosly is a daily mini and midi crossword inspired by The New York Times. Two new puzzles every day. No app, no signup, no tracking. Just open the link.

It's built with the bare minimum — a few static HTML files, React loaded from a CDN, Google Fonts. There's no backend. Your stats live in your own browser. The whole site is open in the URL bar.

I make new puzzles when I have time. If you'd like to suggest one, the email is on the privacy page.
